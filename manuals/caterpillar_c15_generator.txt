CATERPILLAR DIESEL GENERATOR BLOCK SPEC SHEET
MODEL: CATERPILLAR C15 INDUSTRIAL GENERATOR
SECTION 9: OPERATIONS MONITORING & FREQUENCY SAFETY

1. FREQUENCY CRITICAL LIMITS:
* Standard operating frequency: 60.0 Hz.
* Safety tolerance range: 58.5 Hz to 61.5 Hz.
* Critical Frequency Instability threshold: Under 58.0 Hz or Over 62.0 Hz.
* Protocol: Grid frequency instability risks damaging downstream factory systems, inducing server hardware faults, and causing magnetic flux in electric motors.

2. COMPLIANCE & CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES:
If a frequency critical fault is detected on the Caterpillar C15 Generator:
* ACTION 1: Automatically isolate the Caterpillar C15 Generator from the plant's active electrical grid lines (open main breaker).
* ACTION 2: Immediately spin up the backup synchronization turbine (Generator Block B) and match load speed.
* ACTION 3: Dispatch a field service technician with hearing protection and a multitester tool to inspect the electronic governor actuator and fuel line integrity.
